## Objective

Fix three data-layer display bugs in one wave:

1. **BUGFIX-01** — Add a 3rd stat column "SLEEP NEEDED" to `SleepCard` showing `daily?.sleepNeededMin` formatted as "7h 30m".
2. **BUGFIX-02** — Replace `efficiency` with `sleepPerformance` in both `SleepCard.sleepPerformanceLabel` and `RecoveryCard.sleepLabel`. Show "—" when nil. No fallback to `efficiency`.
3. **Dead-code removal** — Delete `SleepView.headlineSection` (a computed property never referenced in `scrollContent`) and its stale TODO comment.
