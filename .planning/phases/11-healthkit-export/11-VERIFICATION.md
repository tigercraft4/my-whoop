### SC-5: SpO₂ export — HK-03 DEFERRED

**Status: DEFERRED — PROTO-11 is HYPOTHESIS**

HK-03 (SpO₂ export via `HKQuantityType(.oxygenSaturation)`) is intentionally omitted from
this phase. PROTO-11 (SpO₂ capture from WHOOP 5.0 hardware) has not been VERIFIED against
a ground-truth oximeter. Implementing HK-03 before PROTO-11 is VERIFIED would export
unvalidated or zero values to Apple Health.

SpO₂ export will be implemented as an incremental phase when PROTO-11 status transitions
from HYPOTHESIS to VERIFIED in a future capture session.

Verification: `grep -r "oxygenSaturation\|spo2\|SpO2" ios/OpenWhoop/HealthKit/` → **0 matches**
